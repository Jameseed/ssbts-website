Company-Specific Production System

Tailored Solutions for Maximum Efficiency

What is a Company-Specific Production System?

Every company has its own set of objectives, resources, and processes. Thus, we design a tailor-made total productive maintenance system that suits your business model seamlessly. Our customized approach ensures maximum efficiency and sustainable improvements.

A Company-Specific Production System integrates various methodologies like Lean, Six Sigma, and Total Productive Maintenance (TPM) to create a cohesive and optimized operational framework. This system is designed to address your unique challenges and leverage your strengths, leading to significant improvements in productivity, quality, and cost-effectiveness.

Our Approach

  • Current State Analysis: We conduct a thorough assessment of your existing production processes to identify inefficiencies, bottlenecks, and areas for improvement.
  • Customized System Design: Based on the analysis, we design a production system that aligns with your strategic goals and operational needs.
  • Implementation & Training: We provide hands-on support during the implementation phase, including training your team on new processes and tools.
  • Performance Monitoring: We establish robust monitoring systems to track key performance indicators (KPIs) and ensure the system delivers expected results.
  • Continuous Improvement: Our systems are designed for continuous evolution, allowing for ongoing optimization and adaptation to changing market conditions.

Benefits of a Tailored Production System

  • Increased operational efficiency and output
  • Reduced waste and production costs
  • Improved product quality and consistency
  • Enhanced employee engagement and safety
  • Greater flexibility and adaptability to market demands
  • Sustainable competitive advantage

"We Are What We Repeatedly Do. Excellence Then, Is Not An Act, But A Habit."
Aristotle (384-322 BC)

Today the above quote from Aristotle is very relevant for Business Excellence journey. Less than 2 % of organizations sustain the journey after 5 years. This is mainly because of Not making excellence as a culture in the organization. Tools can be learnt but culture has to be created with focus and perseverance. Culture can be called soft part of Business Excellence.

Strategies like TQM, Lean and Six Sigma are hard part of Business Excellence. The secret of success is a combination of soft and hard parts.

Combining them together will make the journey not only sustainable but also enjoyable.

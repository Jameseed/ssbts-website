About Us

Driving Excellence, Inspiring Transformation

Our Story

Seven Steps Business Transformation Systems was founded with a singular vision: to empower organizations to achieve sustainable excellence through proven methodologies and transformative strategies. With years of experience in lean manufacturing, quality improvement, and productivity enhancement, our team of dedicated consultants brings unparalleled expertise to every project.

We believe that true business excellence is not just about implementing tools, but about fostering a culture of continuous improvement and innovation. Our approach is holistic, addressing both the 'hard' aspects of process optimization and the 'soft' aspects of leadership development and cultural change.

Our Mission

To guide and support organizations in their journey towards operational and business excellence, enabling them to achieve breakthrough objectives, enhance customer satisfaction, and build a stellar reputation in their respective industries.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and sought-after business transformation partner globally, recognized for our innovative solutions, measurable results, and unwavering commitment to client success.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We are committed to delivering the highest quality of service and achieving outstanding results for our clients.
  • Integrity: We operate with honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all our interactions.
  • Collaboration: We work closely with our clients, fostering strong partnerships and co-creating solutions.
  • Innovation: We continuously seek new and better ways to solve challenges and drive progress.
  • Sustainability: We focus on building capabilities and systems that ensure long-term success for our clients.

"We Are What We Repeatedly Do. Excellence Then, Is Not An Act, But A Habit."
Aristotle (384-322 BC)

Today the above quote from Aristotle is very relevant for Business Excellence journey. Less than 2 % of organizations sustain the journey after 5 years. This is mainly because of Not making excellence as a culture in the organization. Tools can be learnt but culture has to be created with focus and perseverance. Culture can be called soft part of Business Excellence.

Strategies like TQM, Lean and Six Sigma are hard part of Business Excellence. The secret of success is a combination of soft and hard parts.

Combining them together will make the journey not only sustainable but also enjoyable.
